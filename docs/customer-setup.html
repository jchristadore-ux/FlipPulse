Automated 15-Minute BTC Up/Down Trading — Customer Setup Guide
Welcome! Getting your bot running takes about 10 minutes. Just follow the two sections below and send us the items highlighted in green. We handle everything else. You'll start in paper (practice) mode — no real money at risk until you say go.

Part 1 · Kalshi

1
Create an account at kalshi.com. Once it's funded, tell us your starting balance.
2
In Kalshi, go to Account → Security → Create Key.
3
Send us both: the API Key and the PEM key file it gives you.

Part 2 · Telegram

4
Download Telegram, then start a chat with @BotFather.
5
Send him: /newbot
6
Give your bot a name — the username must end in "bot" (e.g. myname_bot).
7
BotFather replies with a token. Send us that token.
8
Start a chat with @userinfobot, say hello, and send us the ID number it gives you back.
Keep it private: your API Key, PEM file, and bot token are like passwords — please send them to us through a secure channel we've agreed on, and don't post them anywhere public.
That's it — once we have your details we'll deploy your FlipPulse bot and confirm it's live in your Telegram.
