Before you begin

Here's the whole picture

FlipPulse is an automated trading bot that runs on Kalshi (a regulated US exchange) and texts you updates through Telegram (a free messaging app). To connect it to your account, you'll collect four things below and paste them into one form at the end. That's it.

The API key only lets the bot place trades on your own Kalshi account — it cannot withdraw funds. Your money never touches us.
